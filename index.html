Frequenty Asked Questions

How much fee I have to deposit?

Please follow link ‘Home-> Fee Details and Important Dates’ for getting your fee details.

I am a USOL/Department/College student then why I should select ‘Student Type’ as private?

A Reappear/deficient/Additional/Improvement exam is always given as Private Student.

I am not able to deposit fee in Bank as they said ‘your record not found’?

. Download Post Office challan and submit your fees in Post Office.

I am not able to upload my Photo & Signature?

Reduce size of your Photo & Signature upto less than 100KB, then try again to upload.

I am unable to download my Admit Card.

If you are a private/USOL student then login into your account with your username and password, and download your admit card. .
